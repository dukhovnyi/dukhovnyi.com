Features

Your Productivity Gets Better And Better Over Time

There are many possibilities for optimization your work with clipboard

Manage pasteboard history

The ability to keep track of everything you copy to the clipboard, so you can easily access and reuse items.

Customizable shortcuts

The ability to set custom keyboard shortcuts to quick access the application and pasteboard history.

Preview functionality

The ability to preview items in your clipboard history, so you can easily identify what you're copying and pasting.

Automatic deletion

The ability to automatically delete items from your clipboard history after a certain period of time or number of uses, to save space and maintain privacy.

Quick access menu

A quick access menu that can be accessed with a keyboard shortcut that allows for easy access to pasteboard history. Also available macOS agent.
